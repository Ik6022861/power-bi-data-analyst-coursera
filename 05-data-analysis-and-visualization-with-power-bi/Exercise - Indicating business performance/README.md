Project Overview

This project analyzes business performance data for Adventure Works using Power BI. The goal was to compute and visualize key performance indicators (KPIs) to help the sales team understand overall performance, sales trends, marketing effectiveness, and regional rankings.

----------------------------
Objectives

The report was designed to answer the following business questions:

What are the total and average sales?

What are the monthly total sales?

How many total orders were placed?

What is the total and monthly marketing expenditure?

How have sales changed over time?

How does sales performance correlate with marketing spend?

Which sales region achieved the highest sales?

How did regional rankings change over time?

How effective were regional advertising campaigns?
